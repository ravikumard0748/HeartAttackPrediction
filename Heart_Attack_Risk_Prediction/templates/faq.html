Frequently Asked Questions

What is CardioXpulse?

CardioXpulse is an advanced heart attack risk prediction system that uses machine learning to assess your risk of experiencing a heart attack based on various health parameters.

How accurate are the predictions?

Our system uses a sophisticated machine learning model trained on extensive medical data. While highly accurate, the predictions should be used as a screening tool and not as a replacement for professional medical advice.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide basic health metrics like age, gender, heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar levels, and specific cardiac markers (CK-MB and Troponin).

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your health information is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal data with third parties.

How often should I check my risk?

We recommend checking your risk whenever you get new health metrics from your doctor. Regular monitoring can help track changes in your heart health over time.

What should I do if I get a high-risk result?

If you receive a high-risk result, don't panic. Consult with your healthcare provider immediately and share your risk assessment results with them for proper medical evaluation.
