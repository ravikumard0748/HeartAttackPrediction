Essential Heart Health Precautions

🏃

Regular Exercise

30 minutes of daily exercise can reduce heart attack risk by 50%. Include both cardio and strength training in your routine.

🥗

Heart-Healthy Diet

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Limit saturated fats and avoid trans fats completely.

🚭

Quit Smoking

Smoking increases heart attack risk by 200%. Quitting can reduce your risk to near non-smoker levels within 5 years.

😴

Quality Sleep

7-9 hours of quality sleep helps maintain heart health. Poor sleep is linked to increased heart disease risk.

🧘‍♂️

Stress Management

Practice meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises. Chronic stress can increase heart attack risk significantly.

🩺

Regular Check-ups

Schedule regular blood pressure, cholesterol, and diabetes screenings. Early detection is key to prevention.

🚰

Stay Hydrated

Drink 8-10 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration helps maintain healthy blood circulation and heart function.

🥑

Heart-Healthy Fats

Include omega-3 rich foods like fish, nuts, and avocados. These fats help reduce inflammation and support heart health.

🧂

Limit Sodium

Keep sodium intake under 2,300mg daily. Excess salt can lead to high blood pressure and increased heart strain.

❤️

Know Your Numbers

Monitor your blood pressure, cholesterol, BMI, and blood sugar regularly. Knowledge is the first step to prevention.
